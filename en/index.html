FAQ

Common questions

How does PesCam record before the strike happens?
PesCam keeps recording continuously in the background. When you press the volume button, it saves the last 10, 15 or 30 seconds — plus what you recorded after that. It is the same technology used by professional action cameras, but on your phone.
Does it work on any Android phone?
Yes. It works on any Android phone running version 7.0 or newer. Most mid-range and flagship phones already support PesCam without issues.
Does it need internet to record?
No. PesCam records 100% offline. Perfect for rivers, lakes and places with no cell signal.
Is it really free?
Yes. Your first 3 videos are completely free, with no signup and no card required. For unlimited recordings, PesCam Premium costs just US$0.99 per month.
What chest mount should I use with PesCam?
Any universal chest mount for smartphones will work. Adjustable models with a secure phone holder are the most recommended.
